For better Exploration - A Travel Meeting App Design

旅游创意项目 - 一款帮助游客结识当地人的Meeting软件

This project was intended to be a app design from start to finish. I was thinking to create a Travel app which can help travellers to better explore the place. At the beginning, I didn't have a concrete idea regarding what exactly the function would be , so I start exploring the users(travellers) requirements. I was inspired by Google's Design Sprint Process, so I tried to go over the process (unfortunately the people whome I was collaberate with remotely didn't seem to work well together) So I continue to work on the idea myself. . Before deciding the final idea, There were several other ideas such as a gaming app which helps users to learn the local language, a calling-assistant app which helps travellers to ask locals for help directly ,an idea about helping to locate wifi spots... After some kind of design iteration, they all turned out not to work well(or probably I didn't have further investment to put into futher improvement). Finally, the meeting app idea seems to both cater the need for travellers' social requirement, and at the same time to help them better understand the culture and the place by connecting with (local) people

这个项目本来是一项用于推向市场的产品创意设计。我想创建一个旅行应用程序，可以帮助旅行者更好地探索这个地方。                           一开始，我对于功能到底是什么还没有一个具体的想法，所以我开始探索用户（旅行者）的需求。我受到了Google的设计冲刺流程的启发，所以我试图通过这个过程（不幸的是，我与远程的人合作似乎并不能很好地协同工作）                         。在决定最终的想法之前，还有其他一些想法，例如帮助用户学习当地语言的游戏应用程序，帮助旅行者直接询问当地人的助手应用程序，以及帮助定位wifi点的想法。 。                          但是后来想到其实最根本的解决方式就是与人交流，通过直接交流的方式可以更有效地获取当地第一手的资讯。                          最后，产生的MeetingPal这种与本地人直接见面的应用设计理念似乎也更迎合旅客的社会需求。通过该方式也可以帮助他们更好地理解当地文化

Audio Workshop -> Idea for Feeling Calligraphy

创意音频工作室

This is the first time we have used the latest technology to help design a workshop. At this symposium, we pre-empt the hand stress of artists based on different situations in their calligraphy exercises and map their Map to the corresponding preset music, each of which is mapped to a specific emotional experience. Then, when the user picks up the sensor pen inside the pen to calligraphy practice, according to different writing intensity will hear different music. In the final presentation, we illustrated the idea of how it's possible to intergrade technology to enhance user experience by showing the workable demos.

这个音频创意交流会在湖南大学深圳媒体实验室举行。 这是我们第一次使用最新的技术协助设计开展的一次工作坊。 在这个研讨会上，我们预设了艺术家进行书法练习时根据不同情景所产生的力量，并将其Map到相应的预设音乐中， 每种预设音乐都映射到特定的情感体验。然后，当用户拿起里面预置传感器的笔来进行书法练习的时候，就会根据书写力度的不同而听到不一样的音乐 。

Dynamic City Scape – A Augmented Reality Mobile App Project

动态城市 - 一款应用增强现实技术 设计的Android 应用

The project leverages augmented reality and Unity as the engine for a mobile application that instantly interacts with attractions. The idea is that visitors use their phone camera to point at a particular attraction, according to the user-defined preferences, the phone displays the corresponding attractions in different seasons or at different times of the day via a holographic 3D image. The user then can have a immersive feeling of their real time environment more compared to when it occurs just the 2D image, which seems more funny. Besides, through the real-time interaction within the field, the user can experience the various sense of that space regardless of the time difference.

该项目利用增强现实技术，并以Unity为引擎开发的一款能与旅游景点做即时交互的手机应用软件。想法是游客将摄像头对准某一景点，就能根据用户自定义的选择，在手机中显示对应景点在不同时节或者在一天中不同时间所呈现的 独特立体景象。用户对现实景区的全方位了解突破了传统的平面海报宣传，通过实地实时的动态交互，在实际和虚拟交错的体验中获取突破了时间局限，能随时体验景点在不一样的时节给人的感受。

Interactive System Design -> An App design which aims to remind elderly to better organize purchased food

交互系统设计- 一款关注老人食品安全的食物保鲜提醒的手机应用

In this project, we developed a mobile phone app to remind elderly people not to eat outdated food. We did not only concern about how people can achieve the task on the app, but also consider how they can easily access to different functions on the app without struggling to read any user manuals. We thought about the problem from a systematic perspective and apply design theories to solve the problem. But first and foremost, the fundamental problem for design is to define user's goal. It's the question regarding why and what they need versus how to achieve the function. We went through the process of Research, Sketch & Ideation, Prototyping with low-fi and hi-fi, User Test and Evaluation. However, due to the time and resource limitation, we didn’t have time to further validate the necessity of the function and make further improvement.

Public Sound Art Installation -> Human Harp Version 3

Human Harp - 伦敦公共声音 交互乐器艺术装置 设计

The project is a public art installation project initialized in London, it’s previously called the “Human Harp”, this is its website: https://humanharp.org/ 。 The ultimate effect is that people can extract the desired sound elements in any environment, and real-time import software to deal with the user through the body movements to coordinate the control of music variables, such as volume, rhythm, whether to join the echo and other special sound effects processing, And Users can feel the music elements changes in respond to body movements in a real environment setup. In short, this is a public art installation that creates music through the interaction of body movements. I was responsible for human harp version 3 design from the idea creation to engineering realization. During the process, I was intentionally exploring the possibility of bridging body interaction with its real live environment and how to make use of sound elements to generate immersive experience.

本项目为一用于公共展示的艺术创意： 表现方式是人们可以在任何的环境中抽取想要的声音元素，并实时导入软件中处理，以实现用身体动作控制音乐变量的实时操纵游戏。 本人负责human harp version 3 从项目的想法创意 到工程技术实现 和艺术包装的系列设计。

Product Concept Design for Haier Leader ‘s second-generation Refrigerator

海尔_新生活第二代冰箱迭代创意

This is another internship project when I worked at ISAR UI design company. It’s a user experience research project where we produce the conceptual product design idea sketch as an ultimate output. This project is an improvement design of a new generation of refrigerator Its concept is targeted at the younger generation after the 1980s. By focusing on the concept of "light fashion, leisurely life", we conduct a thorough user research and design process based on the data and marketing information of the design from the previous generation. The whole project has undergone a relatively complete design process, which including marketing background research, user research (questionnaire surveys, user life style logging, on-spot home interviews), data analysis (using SPSS software for data model analysis, establish of behavior and mental models from target user), product sketch concept output (brainstorming, product modeling), user testing (assessment of the viability of output concepts based on test results) and making suggestions and other recommendations including its refrigerator category, capacity, its inner and outside appearance of features and materials, internal function points, marketing advices for the 2nd generation. During the project, I took part in each process in order to learn, contribute and further understand how a rather comprehensive user research and design model can be applied to a practical enterprise project.

这是我在ISAR界面设计公司实习的另一个用户研究项目。给项目负责产出海尔旗下一个品牌冰箱的第二代概念设计。该概念针对的群体是八十年代后的新生代年轻群体。以『轻时尚、悠生活』为概念的重点开展设计。该项目为新一代产品的改进设计，所以需要对之前一代产品的理念以及市场现状进行调研。 整个项目经历了相对较完整的用户体验研究设计流程：包括企业背景调查，用户研究（问卷调查，用户生活模式的日志记录，入户采访调查），数据分析（用SPSS软件进行数据模型分析，建立用户的行为和心智模式），产品创意输出（头脑风暴，针对需求提出产品概念模型），用户测试，并根据测试结果评估产出概念的可行性，以及对出新一代产品做出冰箱品类、容积段、外观特征及材质、内部功能点、营销建议等的建议。

    • The first project is called the singing box

    The project is a prototype of a used egg shell, with different color and arrangement of led lamps on the box. I used photoresistor and gestures sensing by using Arduino+ programming to control the combination of different lights and music. In addition, using Processing's midi and audio processing library, it simulates a scoreable Karaoke function. Which is: the player imitates the songs provided by the singing system. And the system will match the tempo and pitch of the singer with the melody of the original song. According score will be shown based on the accuracy of pitch, melody and rhythm match with the original song. There are also different lighting effects showcase according to the song input of the user.

    第一个项目叫做singing box， 在玛丽女王学院 的Cruft Festival 上进行了展示。 该项目以废旧的鸡蛋壳盒做原型，在盒子上安装了不同颜色和排布的led灯，利用光敏电阻传感和Arduino + Processing的编程，实现了用手势控制不同灯光和音乐的组合。 此外，利用Processing的midi library 和 audio processing ，实现了模拟出一个可评分的K歌功能，玩家模仿演唱系统提供的歌曲，系统将从演唱者演唱的节拍点和音高跟原歌曲旋律的吻合程度给出评分，并且led灯将根据的分的高低展示出不同的的灯光效果组合。

    • The second project is called the beat box

    In this project, I use MaxMsp and Processing software to handle different interaction states. Five pressure sensitive pieces represent different instrument percussive effects, and I also made a Midi keyboard box that simulates a pentatonic scale. Users can create music by tapping different parts of the box, at the same time, the visual effect corresponding to the music creation is also displayed on the screen. Users can also make improvisational visual music creation based on the feedback from the visual patterns on the screen.

    第二个项目 叫做beat box，是在一个 盒子上贴上一系列压力传感片，并利用MaxMsp 和 Processing 的交互处理， 用不同的压感片代表不同的乐器敲击效果，还有模拟出一个 五声音阶的Midi键盘。用户通过拍击盒子的不同部位，进行音乐创作；同时，在大屏幕上还会显示相应组合所对应的视觉效果，用户也可以根据屏幕的效果,来进行即兴的可视化音乐创作。

Discover Creativity & Technology -> Digital Media Art Documentary

探究创意与技术的关系 - 当代数字艺术纪录片

In this documentary, we took a deep look at how technology and art can influence each other, and explore the impact of the development of contemporary science and technology on current digital art performance. We filmed series of regular filming tracks and embed them a website based on 3D JS page in an attempt to let the audience to explore the content freely. From the perspective of the questioner, experts from different fields are interviewed. We interviewed experts in different fields from the perspective of the questioner, and focused on the topics of artificial intelligence and computational creativity, the documentary analyzes the impact of scientific and technological creativity on contemporary design and art development and put forward the prospect of the active role of design and its application in the field of science and technology in the future.

本项目为在英国伦敦大学玛丽女王学院 硕士研究生 阶段攻读的Digital Media and Arts Technology 课程的一项目。 以纪录片的方式探索当代搞科技发展给艺术设计带来的冲击，我们以提问者的视角采访不同领域的专家,并且以人工智能和computational creativity为主题，剖析了科技创意对当代设计和艺术发展所带来的影响，并就未来科技领域对设计应用发展的积极作用的提出了展望。
